## Strategy: Sequential (Primary First)

The only valid submission approach is **Sequential Primary-First**: Submit to the primary payer first, await their decision, then submit to the secondary payer. This is required for proper Coordination of Benefits (COB) compliance.

## Your Task

Based on the coverage assessments, customize this strategy for this specific case:

1. **Assess the Strategy**:
   - Speed score (1-10): How quickly will we reach a decision?
   - Approval probability (1-10): Given the coverage assessments, how likely is approval?
   - Rework risk (1-10): How likely is additional work needed (appeals, resubmissions)?
   - Patient burden (1-10): How much does this impact the patient's wait time/anxiety?

2. **Customize Strategy Details**:
   - Which payer should be primary and why?
   - Are there specific documentation items to prioritize?
   - Key success criteria
   - Risk mitigation actions
