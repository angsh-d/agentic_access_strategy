## Your Task

Analyze what would likely have happened if each alternative strategy was selected instead. For each alternative:

1. **Projected Timeline**: How long would it take to reach a decision?
2. **Projected Outcome**: What would the likely result be?
3. **Key Risks**: What could go wrong with this approach?
4. **Tradeoffs**: What would be gained/lost compared to the selected strategy?
