1. **Opening Statement**: How should the physician introduce the case?

2. **Key Clinical Arguments**: What are the 3-5 strongest clinical points to make?

3. **Anticipated Objections**: What will the medical director likely push back on?

5. **Policy Alignment**: How does the case meet specific policy criteria?
